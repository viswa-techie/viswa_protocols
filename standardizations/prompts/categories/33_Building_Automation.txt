BUILDING AUTOMATION PROTOCOLS:
  ASHRAE BACnet (ISO 16484-5:2017) — Building Automation and Control Networks
      BACnet/IP (Annex J), BACnet MS/TP (RS-485), BACnet Secure Connect (BACnet/SC)
      Object types: Analog/Binary/Multi-state Input/Output/Value, Schedule, Calendar
      BACnet Testing Laboratories (BTL) certification
  KNX (ISO/IEC 14543-3:2006)    — European home/building automation standard
      Wired (TP, PL, RF) and IP-based KNX
      KNX Association certification program
  LonWorks (ISO/IEC 14908)      — Local Operating Network (Echelon)
  Modbus TCP (for BAS)          — Legacy HVAC controller integration
  DALI (IEC 62386)              — Digital Addressable Lighting Interface
  EnOcean (ISO/IEC 14543-3-10)  — Energy harvesting wireless standard
  Thread (IEEE 802.15.4e)       — IP-based mesh for BAS devices
  Matter (CSA/Connectivity Stds Alliance) — Consumer IoT + BAS convergence
  OPC UA (IEC 62541)            — Data exchange in BAS (Siemens, ABB integration)

ENERGY MANAGEMENT IN BUILDINGS:
  ISO 50001:2018                — Energy management systems
  EN 15232 (EPBD)               — Energy performance of buildings (BAS impact)
  ASHRAE Standard 90.1          — Energy Standard for Buildings (USA)
  ASHRAE Standard 55            — Thermal environmental conditions
  IEC 61970/61968 (CIM)         — Common Information Model (BAS-to-grid gateway)
  BREEAM (UK Green Building)    — Building Research Establishment Environmental Assessment
  LEED (US Green Building)      — Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design
