EQUIPMENT COMMUNICATION (SECS/GEM):
  SEMI E4 (SECS-I)             — SEMI Equipment Communication Standard (serial)
  SEMI E5 (SECS-II)            — Message content and format
  SEMI E30 (GEM)               — Generic Equipment Model (core automation model)
  SEMI E37 (HSMS)              — High-Speed Message Services (TCP/IP replacement for SECS-I)
  SEMI E39 (OSMS)              — Object Services Model
  SEMI E40 (PJM)               — Processing Job Management
  SEMI E87 (CMS)               — Carrier Management System (FOUP/cassette)
  SEMI E90 (STS)               — Substrate Tracking Standard
  SEMI E94 (CJM)               — Control Job Management
  SEMI E116 (EqPM)             — Equipment Performance Management
  SEMI E132 (SECSGEM.NET)      — .NET implementation
  SEMI E137                    — Equipment Self-Description

MATERIAL HANDLING & AUTOMATION:
  SEMI E84                     — Enhanced Parallel I/O Interface (passive handoff)
  SEMI E87                     — Carrier Management (FOUP-to-loadport)
  SEMI E23                     — Mechanical specification for cassettes
  SEMI M1 series               — Silicon wafer standards (dimensions, bow, warp)
  SEMI E58 (AMHS)              — Automated Material Handling Systems
  SEMI E167                    — AMHS 450mm wafer guidelines

EQUIPMENT RELIABILITY & OEE:
  SEMI E10-0812                — Metrics for Fab Equipment OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness)
      States: Equipment Down, Scheduled Down, Unscheduled Down, Idle,
              Productive, Engineering, Non-Scheduled
  SEMI E35                     — Cost of Ownership Metrics
  SEMI E79                     — Overall Equipment Effectiveness for semiconductor
  SEMI E116                    — Equipment Performance Management

CYBERSECURITY FOR FAB:
  SEMI E187 (2022)             — Specification for Cybersecurity of Fab Equipment
      Asset management, threat detection, authentication, network segmentation
  SEMI E188 (2022)             — Malware Free Equipment Integration
      Pre-delivery scanning, patch management, incident response
  SEMI CS-0 through CS-4       — Cybersecurity security levels for equipment

CLEANROOM & CONTAMINATION:
  ISO 14644-1:2015             — Cleanrooms: Classification of air cleanliness by particle
      ISO Class 1 through 9 (replaces US Fed Std 209E)
  ISO 14644-2:2015             — Monitoring to provide evidence of cleanroom performance
  SEMI E78                     — Guide to Assess and Control Electrostatic Discharge (ESD)
  SEMI F47                     — Specification for semiconductor processing equipment:
                                  voltage sag immunity
  SEMI S2-0706E                — Environmental, Health and Safety Guideline for Semiconductor Equipment
  SEMI S8-0706                 — Safety guidelines for ergonomics

ADVANCED PACKAGING & 3D IC:
  SEMI P47                     — Specification for reticle handling
  SEMI E167                    — 450mm AMHS guidelines
  JEDEC JESD22-B112A           — Package warpage measurement
  IPC-7095C                    — Design/Assembly Process for BGA packages
  SEMI 3DS (3D Stacking)       — Through-Silicon Via (TSV) standards
  IEEE 1838                    — Die, Stack, System Test

ENVIRONMENTAL & SAFETY:
  SEMI S2                      — Environmental, Health, Safety (EH&S) for equipment
  SEMI S14                     — Fire risk assessment
  SEMI S8                      — Ergonomics
  SEMI S10                     — Risk assessment for semiconductor equipment
  OSHA 1910.119                — Process Safety Management (hazardous materials)
  IEC 61010-1                  — Safety for lab/measurement/control equipment
