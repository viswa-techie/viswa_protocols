HISTORICAL TIMELINE:
  1926 — Air Commerce Act (US): first aviation regulation
  1944 — ICAO Chicago Convention: international aviation law
  1958 — FAA established (US Civil Aeronautics Act)
  1967 — Apollo 1 fire: NASA safety culture reform
  1974 — DO-178: first avionics software standard
  1982 — DO-178A: updated avionics software
  1985 — Therac-25: software safety awareness
  1992 — DO-178B: major revision (until 2011)
  1994 — DO-254: hardware design assurance
  1996 — Ariane 5 Flight 501: software reuse without validation
  2001 — ARP4754 published (systems level)
  2010 — ARP4754A (systems), ARP4761 updated
  2011 — DO-178C published (replaces DO-178B)
  2012 — DO-331/332/333 supplements
  2018 — DO-326A/DO-356A security
  2019 — Boeing 737 MAX crash: MCAS analysis, certification reform
  2023 — ARP4761A (updated safety methods)
  2024 — EASA AI in aviation guidelines; DO-178C for ML guidance drafts
