STANDARDS UNIVERSE:
EU GDPR:
  GDPR (Regulation 2016/679)    — EU General Data Protection Regulation
      Principles (Art. 5): lawfulness, fairness, transparency; purpose limitation;
                            data minimisation; accuracy; storage limitation; integrity & confidentiality
      Legal bases (Art. 6): consent, contract, legal obligation, vital interests, public task, legitimate interest
      Data subject rights (Art. 15-22): access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection, automated decisions
      DPA (Data Protection Authority) enforcement; Art. 77-84 remedies
      Controller vs. Processor distinction; DPA notifications
      DPIA (Data Protection Impact Assessment): Art. 35 for high-risk processing
      BCR (Binding Corporate Rules): cross-border transfer mechanism
      SCCs (Standard Contractual Clauses): EU → third country transfer
      Fines: €20M or 4% global annual turnover (whichever higher)
  ePrivacy Directive 2002/58/EC  — Cookie consent; electronic marketing
  ePrivacy Regulation (proposed) — Update to ePrivacy Directive (still pending 2025)

US PRIVACY:
  CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act, 2018, eff. 2020) — California residents' rights
  CPRA (California Privacy Rights Act, 2020, eff. 2023) — Enhanced CCPA; CPPA (enforcement agency)
      Rights: know, delete, opt-out of sale/sharing, correct, limit use of sensitive PI
  Virginia CDPA (2021)          — Consumer Data Protection Act (effective 2023)
  Colorado CPA (2021)           — Colorado Privacy Act
  Connecticut CTDPA (2022)      — Data protection
  Texas TDPSA (2023)            — Texas Data Privacy and Security Act
  US Federal: No unified national privacy law as of 2024 (ADPPA stalled)
  COPPA (1998)                  — Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (<13 years)
  HIPAA Privacy Rule (1996)     — Healthcare PHI (Protected Health Information)
  GLBA                           — Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act: financial data

ISO/IEC PRIVACY STANDARDS:
  ISO/IEC 27701:2019            — Privacy Information Management System (PIMS)
      Extension of ISO 27001/27002 for PII privacy
      Maps to GDPR, CCPA, APP (Australia), PIPEDA (Canada)
  ISO/IEC 29100:2011+AMD1:2018  — Privacy Framework: principles, actors, interactions
  ISO/IEC 29101:2013            — Privacy Reference Architecture
  ISO/IEC 29134:2023            — Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA) guidelines
  ISO/IEC 29151:2017            — PII protection best practices
  ISO/IEC 29184:2020            — Online privacy notice and consent
  ISO/IEC 27550:2019            — Privacy engineering in systems lifecycle
  ISO/IEC CD TS 27560           — Consent record management

NIST PRIVACY FRAMEWORK:
  NIST Privacy Framework 1.0 (2020) — Identify-P, Govern-P, Control-P, Communicate-P, Protect-P
  NIST SP 800-188               — De-identification of government data sets
  NIST IR 8062                  — Privacy Engineering Objectives
  NIST SP 800-122               — Protecting PII in federal information systems

GLOBAL PRIVACY LAWS:
  PIPEDA (Canada, 2000)         — Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act
  CASL (Canada, 2014)           — Anti-spam; consent for commercial messages
  LGPD (Brazil, Lei 13.709/2018) — General Data Protection Law (effective 2020/2021)
  APPI (Japan, 2003, rev 2022) — Act on Protection of Personal Information (major 2022 amendments)
  PDPL (Saudi Arabia, 2022)     — Personal Data Protection Law (effective 2024)
  POPIA (South Africa, 2013, eff. 2021) — Protection of Personal Information Act
  PDPA (Thailand, 2019, eff. 2022) — Personal Data Protection Act
  PDPA Singapore (2012, rev 2021) — Personal Data Protection Act
  PIPAs (South Korea PIPA, 2011 rev 2023) — Personal Information Protection Act
  China PIPL (2021)             — Personal Information Protection Law (extraterritorial)
  India DPDPA (2023)            — Digital Personal Data Protection Act (notified 2024 implementation)

SPECIAL CATEGORIES OF DATA:
  GDPR Art. 9                   — Special categories: health, biometric, genetic, racial, religious, political
  Biometric standards: ISO/IEC 30136 — Performance testing of biometric verification
  GDPR + biometrics: workplace authentication legal basis
  Facial recognition: EDPB guidelines; EU AI Act prohibition (public real-time)

PRIVACY ENGINEERING PRINCIPLES:
  Privacy by Design (Ann Cavoukian, 7 principles)
  Data minimization (GDPR Art. 5c)
  Anonymization vs. pseudonymization (GDPR recitals 26/29)
  k-anonymity, l-diversity, t-closeness, differential privacy
  Federated learning: privacy-preserving ML

  1973 — US HEW Secretary's Advisory Committee: Fair Information Practices (FIPPs)
  1981 — Council of Europe Convention 108: first international privacy treaty
  1980 — OECD Privacy Guidelines (8 principles)

  2016 — GDPR published (EU 2016/679); enforcement 2018
  2018 — CCPA passed (California); GDPR enforcement begins

  2021 — PDPA Thailand; China PIPL; POPIA South Africa

  2023 — CPRA enforcement; India DPDPA; EU-US Data Privacy Framework (DPF)
  2024 — PDPL Saudi enforcement; India DPDPA implementation; AI Act privacy provisions
