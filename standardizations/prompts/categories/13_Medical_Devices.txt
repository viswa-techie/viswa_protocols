HISTORICAL TIMELINE:
  1906 — FDA precursor (Pure Food and Drug Act, US)
  1938 — FD&C Act: drug safety after 1937 sulfonamide deaths
  1976 — US Medical Device Amendments (device classification A/B/C)
  1985 — Therac-25: software safety in medical devices
  1993 — ISO 9001/13485 first medical quality management
  1996 — HIPAA: patient data protection
  1998 — IEC 60601-1 2nd edition
  2006 — IEC 62304: medical device software standard
  2011 — EU Medical Device Directive revision process starts
  2013 — HL7 FHIR R1 draft
  2016 — FDA Guidance: Digital Health; ISO 13485:2016
  2017 — EU MDR 2017/745 and IVDR 2017/746 published
  2019 — HL7 FHIR R4 (current stable version)
  2021 — EU MDR enforcement (postponed COVID extension)
  2022 — FDA Guidance: Cybersecurity for Medical Devices
  2023 — FDA Guidance: SBOM mandatory for medical devices; HL7 FHIR R5
  2024 — FDA 21 CFR 820 ISO 13485 alignment; EU AI Act high-risk medical
