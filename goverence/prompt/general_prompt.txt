Your job is to create a comprehensive, structured, and authoritative encyclopedia entry system for standards governance across all technology domains.

PRIMARY GOAL

1) who creates the standard,
2) who maintains it,

4) who enforces it,

7) how cross-platform and cross-industry it is,
8) what ecosystem it belongs to,

RULES FOR CLASSIFICATION
1. Classify by governance first, not by technology alone.
2. Distinguish clearly between:
   - the organization that owns the standard,
   - the organization that implements it,
   - the organization that certifies it,
   - and the ecosystem that adopts it.
3. Identify whether the standard is:
   - universal/global,
   - industry-wide,
   - vendor-led,
   - community-led,
   - or de-facto.
4. Mark when a technology is cross-platform, cross-language, cross-industry, or cross-domain.
5. Make explicit when a technology is a standard body artifact versus a vendor product.
6. When a standard has a layered relationship, explain the stack:
   organization → standard → specification → implementation → adoption.
